Overview

The Great Wall of China, one of the most iconic landmarks in the world, stretches over 13,000 miles and was originally built to protect Chinese states from invasion. Today, it stands as a symbol of Chinaâ€™s rich history and is a must-see destination for travelers.

Attractions

Badaling

Badaling is the most visited section of the Great Wall, located just 70 kilometers from Beijing. It offers well-preserved paths and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Mutianyu

Mutianyu is another popular section, famous for its breathtaking scenery and well-restored watchtowers. This area is less crowded than Badaling, making it a great alternative for visitors.

Jinshanling

Jinshanling offers a more rugged and authentic experience, with steep climbs and partially restored sections. It's a favorite for hikers and adventure seekers.
